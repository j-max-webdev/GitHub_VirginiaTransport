Virginia Transport is a dedicated 3PL provider that focuses on both shippers' and carriers' needs. We are committed to finding the right equipment for our customers' freight from a qualified network of national and regional carriers. When VT accepts a load, it gets moved on time and securely no matter what. It is our responsibility and no one else's.

Philosophy

At Virginia Transport, we have created a collaborative, transparent, and open dialogue between ourselves, our shippers, and our carriers. We want our shippers to know that once we commit to a load, they no longer have to worry about it. We recognize that in this industry, a number of issues can occur, and we will take care of them no matter the difficulty or cost. We believe that this dedication is the foundation for long-lasting professional relationships.
