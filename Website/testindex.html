Virginia Transport provides efficient, cost-effective coordination of goods transportation. Our philosophy is bound to our identity as a small business. Our leadership team works directly with our shippers and distributors, ensuring that shipments are effectively carried out to each party's standards. As experienced 3PL providers, we pair shippers with trusted carriers who will deliver goods securely, on schedule, and in perfect condition.

Virginia Transport welcomes inquiries from independent contractors looking to carry loads. We cater to a wide range of industries that ship a variety of goods. Please contact us via the form found at the bottom of the page. Make sure to include information about your carrying capabilities, and we will be in touch with you shortly.

Our Capabilities Include:


Dry Van:
Power Only, Drop Trailer, Multi-Pick/Stop, Floor Loading

Flatbed:
Flatbed, Stepdeck, Conestoga, Extended deck

Intermodal:
Multiple modes of transportation with longer transit time

Drayage:
Customer to terminal/Terminal to customer

Dedicated:
Long Term, high and low volume

Partial Truckload and Volume LTL:
Less than a full truckload.

Temp Controlled:
Refrigerated trucking, FTL, LTL

Open Deck:
Freight that does not fit into traditional enclosed trailer. Heavy Haul, Over Dimensional
