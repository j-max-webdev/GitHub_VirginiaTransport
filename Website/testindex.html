Philosophy

At Virginia Transport, we have created a collaboration, transparency, and open dialog between ourselves, our shippers, and our carriers. We want out shippers to know that once we commit to a load, they no longer have to worry about it. We recognize that in this industry, a number of issues can occur, and we will take care of them no matter the difficulty or cost. We believe that this dedication is the foundation for long-lasting professional relationships.
