Virginia Transport is a dedicated 3-PL provider that focuses on both shippers' and carriers' needs. We work hard to find the right equipment for our customers' freight from a qualified network of national and regional carriers. When VT commits to a load, it gets moved on time and securely no matter what. It is our responsibility and no one else's.
Our team of expeerienced professionals provide unmatched service 24/7/365, and ensure there are no disruptions or gaps in the information stream for any load.
We will work hard to coordinate your delivery to your specifications.
We provide a variety of shipping options: LTL, reefer, van, flatbed. Whatever you need, we will work hard to deliver.

Virginia Transport provides efficient, cost-effective coordination of goods transportation. Our philosophy is bound to our identity as a small business. Our leadership team works directly with our shippers and distributors, ensuring that shipments are effectively carried out to each party's standards. As experienced 3PL providers, we pair shippers with trusted carriers who will deliver goods securely, on schedule, and in perfect condition.
